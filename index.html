Our Certifications

Gemological Institute of America

The Gemological Institute of America (GIA) is recognized worldwide as the foremost authority in gemology. Their diamond grading reports are the industry standard for diamond quality assurance and authenticity.

International Gemological Institute

The International Gemological Institute (IGI) is a global leader in diamond certification and jewelry appraisal. Their comprehensive evaluations provide detailed assessments of a diamond's quality and characteristics.
